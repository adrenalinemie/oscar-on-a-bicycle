☆ Oscar

This little toddler has been on the wheels pretty much since his birth. He is super enthusiastic and easy going during our trips. It is amazing to see this little person growing and becoming a confident, funny, curious and clever little boy.  We really want to live this adventure as a family and Oscar is one of our main reasons for this Journey. We can buy lots of things in life, but we cannot buy time (as far as we know), and the time with Oscar is gold. While we are on the road, we also want to teach him important values such as open-mindedness, confidence, patience... Obviously, we sometimes have mishaps on the road with a toddler (do you want to hear the story of the hot chocolate poured in the tent while it was raining outside?), however it is clearly balanced with numerous enjoyable moments and positive impacts.
